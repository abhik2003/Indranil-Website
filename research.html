RESEARCH



My research interest lies in the broad area of quantum computing and quantum communication


In my PhD I am going to explore “ Secure quantum communication in multiplexed optical networks ”


In this project we will comprehensively study and develop quantum communication protocols towards robust and efficient implementation in the multiplexed optical networks.This project will enable research in two main approaches to quantum communication, namely discrete and continuous quantum variables, which rely respectively on particle- and wave-type properties of quantum light. By revealing the advantages and weaknesses of each of the approaches, taking into account practical imperfections of channels and devices, we will set benchmarks for entanglement distribution and quantum key distribution (QKD) performance over multiplexed networks and come up with the solutions, likely combining both approaches, for practical networked secure quantum communication.
