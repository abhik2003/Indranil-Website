ABOUT



I completed my BSc (2016-2019) and MSc (2019-2021) in Physics from Visva-Bharati, a central university in India with a specialization in Quantum Electronics and Atomic-Molecular Physics and elective in ‘Topics in Modern Quantum Mechanics’.

After completing my masters’ I have worked with Prof. Prasanta K Panigrahi, Dept. of Physics, IISER Kolkata on ‘quantum information theory’. Then, I joined Dr. Anindita Banerjee at C-DAC to work on “quantum source for free-space quantum communication”. I gained more experience in the field of quantum computing after joining Prof. Ankur Raina, IISER Bhopal. There I worked on “measurement-based quantum computing”
